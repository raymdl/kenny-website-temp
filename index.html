About Me

Turning ideas into motion

Hey, I'm Kenny — a motion graphics designer with a BFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago (2017). After spending four years honing my craft in Chicago, I relocated to Los Angeles in 2021 to push the boundaries of animation and motion graphics on the West Coast.

I help people and companies communicate and express their ideas in a fun, visual, and entertaining way — whether that's a slick brand video, an immersive 3D piece, or a punchy social campaign.

Every project starts with a conversation and ends with something that moves — literally.
